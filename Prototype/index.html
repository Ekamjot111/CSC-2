What is Basketball?

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players. The objective of the game is to score more points than the opposing team by shooting the ball through a 10-foot-high ring known as the hoop. Players must use skill, speed, teamwork, and strategy to outplay their opponents.

History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts. It was originally created as an indoor winter sport to keep athletes active during colder months. Over time, the sport expanded rapidly across the world and is now one of the most popular and widely played sports internationally.

Basic Basketball Rules

  • Each team has five players on the court.
  • Players advance the ball by passing or dribbling.
  • A shot inside the three-point line is worth two points.
  • A shot outside the three-point line is worth three points.
  • Free throws are worth one point.
