NBA Legends

Player: Photo: Team: Fun fact:
Michael Jordan Chicago Bulls Michael Jordan was cut from his high school basketball team in his sophomore year. This motivated him to train harder and eventually become one of the greatest basketball players of all time.
LeBron James Los Angeles Lakers His nickname was "The Chosen One" in high school and he went directly to the NBA without playing college basketball.
Stephen Curry Golden State Warriors Stephen Curry revolutionised basketball by making long-distance three-point shooting a major strategy in the NBA.
